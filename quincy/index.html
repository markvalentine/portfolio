Quincy Brave Conway

Dear friends and family of Quincy Brave Conway,

We have made this page for Quincy in the hopes to give people a space to share their memories of him, the impact he had, and who he was for so many people.

We have a rough phone application that we hope will be a tool in celebrating and remembering his life - if you have an iPhone/iPad, please sign up using the link at the bottom.

Otherwise, feel free to use this page, which we made in an effort to make it easier for Quincy's friends and family to share their thoughts.

We plan to gift all of these memories to the family at a later date in a presentable book form. We will also make sure that what you share here is preserved for you to revisit and cherish.

We're new at this, and ask for some forgiveness in using both the app and this site, as it is still in the works. We are a small group of individuals dedicated to helping people celebrate the lives of loved ones. Some of us attended SFPrep, and we've gone through similar life experiences; Mikahla helped many of us through the process, and we hope we can return the favor now to all of Quincy's friends and family.

Our thoughts are with you all.
